Our Mission

TechNova's mission is to enrich the community by delivering hands-on STEM education to children of all ages. We believe in cultivating diversity from a young age, striving to ensure every learner, particularly young women, has the tools to become a confident technical leader.

What We Do

From foundational coding workshops and hands-on programming projects to guest speaker panels and mentorship opportunities, TechNova provides hands-on experiences that spark curiosity and build real skills. Members collaborate, learn, and grow together while exploring a wide range of STEM fields.

About Us

TechNova was born from a passion for closing the gender gap in STEM fields. Our core belief is in the need for a supportive, inclusive, and challenging environment where future leaders can thrive.

Our organization is powered by dedicated student volunteers and supported by professional mentors from the tech industry.
