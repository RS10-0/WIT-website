Our Mission

TechNova's mission is to enrich the community by delivering hands-on STEM education to children of all ages. We believe in cultivating diversity from a young age, striving to ensure every learner, particularly young women, has the tools to become a confident technical leader.

What We Do

From foundational coding workshops and hands-on programming projects to guest speaker panels and mentorship opportunities, TechNova provides hands-on experiences that spark curiosity and build real skills. Members collaborate, learn, and grow together while exploring a wide range of STEM fields.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do workshops work?

We host specialized workshops approximately 1-2 times per month. These events are designed to offer either an intensive mini-course on an advanced topic or a unique opportunity to connect with and learn from amazing local industry leaders and guest speakers.

Are the classes free?

Yes, all classes, workshops, and learning resources provided by TechNova are completely free of charge. Our mission is to ensure all youth have equal opportunities to explore technology and coding without financial barriers.

Will my child learn Java coding or block coding?

Choice is encouraged! We proudly offer separate curriculums for both Java (text-based) and block coding. Parents and students can choose the path that best suits their learning goals. Please explore our Resources section for more in-depth details on each curriculum.

Why Java?

Java is an industry-standard, object-oriented programming language that powers billions of devices and major enterprise systems. Learning Java provides a strong foundation in computer science fundamentals, prepares students for AP Computer Science courses, and equips them with a highly marketable and scalable skill set for future careers in tech.

My kid doesn't know anything about programming, can they join?

Absolutely. TechNova is built to welcome complete beginners. Our programs are specifically structured to introduce fundamental concepts in a supportive, low-pressure environment, ensuring a positive starting experience for every student.

Where do I sign up?

To enroll, simply scroll to the top of the page and click the "Join Our Community" button. You will be directed to a sign-up form. Following your submission, you will receive a confirmation email with class details for your child within 1-2 business days.

Weekly Classes

Register for Classes →

We offer weekly classes for all ages, designed to fit your child's learning style. We offer two different curriculums: Java Basics, which teaches the fundamentals of text-based, object-oriented programming, and Block Coding Fundamentals, which provides a visual, drag-and-drop introduction to logic and problem-solving.

Monthly Workshops

We typically host 1-2 workshops every month. These events can range anywhere from 2 to 5 days, depending on the topic. Workshops often include guest speakers from the industry, opportunities to learn new developmental skills, and other exciting opportunities, so stay on the lookout!
