Our Mission

TechNova's mission is to enrich the community by delivering hands-on STEM education to children of all ages. We believe in cultivating diversity from a young age, striving to ensure every learner, particularly young women, has the tools to become a confident technical leader.

Why TechNova?

We aren't just a coding club. We are a launchpad for the next generation of digital pioneers.

🤝

Community First

Our members don't just sit in lectures; they build together, fail together, and succeed together through peer learning.

Hands-on Growth

🌍

Radical Inclusion

Bridging the gender gap in tech is in our DNA. We create a safe, high-energy space for everyone to lead.

Built by Mentors, for Students
