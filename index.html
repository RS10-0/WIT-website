Our Mission

Women in Tech empowers young women and allies to explore STEM, build skills, and connect with a supportive community. Through workshops, mentorship, and hands-on projects, we aim to inspire curiosity, confidence, and innovation in the next generation of tech leaders.

What We Do

From coding workshops and robotics projects to guest speaker panels and mentorship opportunities, Women in Tech provides hands-on experiences that spark curiosity and build real skills. Members collaborate, learn, and grow together while exploring a wide range of STEM fields.
